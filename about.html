About Us

Welcome to Zama-Stores, your neighborhood mall — now just a few taps away. We’re redefining convenience by bringing the mall experience directly to your door. Whether you need groceries, gadgets, clothing, or home essentials, Zama-Stores connects you to nearby sellers for fast, reliable delivery.

At Zama-Stores, we believe shopping should be effortless. That’s why our platform is designed to make every order as easy and fast as possible. We prioritize proximity — so your products come from the nearest source, helping you save time and enjoy faster service.

We’re not just delivering products — we’re delivering ease, access, and trust.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s most accessible, reliable, and community-powered digital mall — delivering convenience at the shortest route possible.

Our Mission

  • To offer fast, easy access to products through hyper-local delivery.

  • To simplify the shopping experience with a seamless, mobile-first platform.

  • To bridge the gap between physical malls and digital convenience.

  • To empower local sellers by extending their reach in real time.

  • To continuously innovate ways to reduce delivery time and enhance customer satisfaction.

Our Core Values

  • Proximity: We shorten the gap between you and what you need.
  • Ease: Simple shopping for everyone, anytime.
  • Speed: We minimize delivery time through smart routing.
  • Trust: Every transaction is secure, every order reliable.
  • Community: We grow with local merchants and users alike.
