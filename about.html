About Us

Welcome to Zama-Stores, your neighborhood mall — now just a few taps away. We’re redefining convenience by bringing the mall experience directly to your door. Whether you need groceries, gadgets, clothing, or home essentials, Zama-Stores connects you to nearby sellers for fast, reliable delivery.

At Zama-Stores, we believe shopping should be effortless. That’s why our platform is designed to make every order as easy and fast as possible. We prioritize proximity — so your products come from the nearest source, helping you save time and enjoy faster service.

We’re not just delivering products — we’re delivering ease, access, and trust.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Zama-Stores?

Zama-Stores is a digital version of your local mall that allows users to shop conveniently from multiple vendors and have items delivered using the shortest and fastest possible route.

How fast is delivery?

Our system is designed to optimize delivery speed by matching orders with the closest vendors and dispatching items using the most efficient local routes — often within hours.

Can I shop from multiple stores at once?

Yes! You can add products from different vendors within the Zama-Stores platform to your cart and enjoy combined or individual deliveries, depending on availability and location.

Is there a delivery fee?

Delivery fees are affordable and dynamically calculated based on distance and vendor, with many vendors offering free or discounted delivery promotions.

How do I track my order?

You’ll receive real-time updates through your order dashboard. You can also opt in for SMS and email notifications for every major delivery step.

What if I need to return an item?

Returns are easy. Each vendor has a return policy listed on their store page, and our team is available to help you schedule a pickup or guide you through the return process.
