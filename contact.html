Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I reach Zama-Stores support?

You can contact us directly through our contact form or email. We respond quickly, usually within a few hours.

What are your customer service hours?

Our support team is active from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday to Saturday. For urgent issues, we offer limited support on Sundays.

Can I speak directly to a delivery agent?

Yes. After placing an order, you’ll get an option to contact your assigned delivery person for updates.

I have a problem with my order. What do I do?

Contact us immediately with your order ID. We’ll investigate and resolve the issue as fast as possible.

Do you support vendors too?

Absolutely. If you're a seller or vendor, our support line is open to help you manage listings, logistics, and payments.
